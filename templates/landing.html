TAAMS Journal

The Akungba Administrators and Management Scientists (TAAMS) is a peer review Journal of the Faculty of Administration and Management Sciences, Adekunle Ajasin University, Akungba Akoko, Ondo State Nigeria.

It is a multidisciplinary journal with focus on Administration and Management Sciences researchers.

The journal provides an excellent platform for the exchange of ideas among researches in Accounting, Banking and Finance, Business Administration, Public Administration and other Management, Social and Behavioural Sciences. The journal publishes twice each year in May and September.
